Privacy

Minimal data. No gameplay recording.

Last updated July 24, 2026. This page describes the current early-access privacy posture.

What We Collect

For licensing, ClickAssist sends your license key, purchase email, app version, and a hashed device identifier to the license server. Stripe handles payment data; ClickAssist does not store card numbers.

What We Do Not Collect

ClickAssist does not record gameplay, screenshots, keystroke text, chat messages, files, browser history, or game account credentials.

Local Runtime

The click engine runs locally on your Windows device. Network access is used for license activation, license refresh, checkout, downloads, and future update checks.
